What is AWW?

AWW is an interactive game built on a web platform that encourages youths aged 18 to 27 to serve others through words of affirmation, encouragement and acts of service

AWW's Vision

To be a leading icon in the facilitation of spreading good in Singapore.

AWW's Mission

To inspire and create a compassionate and generous community by inculcating habits of kindness through actions and words.
