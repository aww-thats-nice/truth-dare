How to Play?

This game has three main functionalities

Card Deck

Card deck is a virtual card game to be played in a group setting. Take turns to draw cards from the deck and answer the questions or carry out the tasks accordingly.

Randomizer

Use randomizer if you're feeling spontaneous for the day! It'll give you a place to start and an idea of how you can make someone smile this week.

Bingo

Are you up for this challenge? Complete this bingo card with your friends in two weeks! (Note: Each task only needs to be completed by one person) Login to participate here.
