How to Play?

This game has three main functionalities

Card Deck

Card deck is a virtual group card game! Sit in a circle and take turns drawing card from the deck. Answer the questions on the card or do the things as instructed

Randomizer

Use randomizer if you're feeling spontaneous for the day! It'll give you a place to start and an idea of what kind acts can you do this week

Bingo

Use this bingo card to do 25 kind words or actions, you could do it yourself or with your friends! Sign up for an account to participate here
